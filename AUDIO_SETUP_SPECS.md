## System Integration & Connections

### Signal Flow
1. **Wireless Microphone** → **Yamaha MG10XU Mixer** (via receiver output)
2. **Yamaha MG10XU Mixer** → **M-Audio 1000 Amplifier** (via main outputs)
3. **M-Audio 1000 Amplifier** → **Kevler ZLX 15 Speakers** (Left & Right channels)

### Connection Notes
- **Important:** Verify if Kevler ZLX 15 speakers are active (built-in amplifier) or passive. If active, the M-Audio 1000 amplifier may not be needed, or the connection will differ.
- For active speakers: Mixer → Active Speakers (via XLR or TRS cables)
- For passive speakers: Mixer → Power Amplifier → Passive Speakers (via speaker cables)
- Wireless microphone receiver connects to one of the mixer's mono input channels

### Recommended Cables
- XLR cables for microphone inputs
- TRS or XLR cables for mixer-to-amplifier connection
- Speaker cables (if using passive speakers) from amplifier to speakers
- USB cable for mixer-to-computer connection (optional)
